in light of casey affleck and ex-wife summer phoenix finalizing their divorce, court documents have emerged revealing afflecks salary.

according to a court filing obtained by people, affleck earns $400,000 per year. phoenix, who works as an actress and model, earns about $24,000 per year.

the manchester by the sea oscar winner and phoenix share two children, 13-year-old indiana and 9-year-old atticus.

affleck will pay his ex-wife $4,000 per month in child support and for their childrens private school tuition for the next two years, people reported. he will not be paying spousal support.

the former couple filed for divorce last august after nearly 10 years of marriage and 16 years together.
