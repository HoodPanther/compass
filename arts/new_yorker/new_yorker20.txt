in the wake of the avalanche of claims against harvey weinstein , more and more powerful men across the nationin entertainment, newsrooms, tech firms, and politicsare being accused of sexual harassment, and are being called to account. ronan farrow reported, for the new yorker , some of the most shocking charges , including rape, that women in the entertainment industry have made against weinstein. (weinstein has denied claims of nonconsensual sex.) farrow also detailed a campaign of intimidation and threats that weinstein waged against accusers. alexandra schwartz has been exploring how the scandal is rippling through our society and on social media. the two writers spoke with david remnick about how the escalating movement seems to be changing how america thinks about sexual misconduct.
