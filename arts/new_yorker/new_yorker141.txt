the horn, uniquely welcome in both the brass and the woodwind families, is a brash but finicky device: for centuries the official instrument of the hunt, it is also capable of melting lyricism. the four virtuosos of genghis barbie, which calls itself the leading post post-feminist feminist all-female horn experience, give that heritage an irreverent post-classical twist. theyll offer an ugly holiday sweater party at miller theatre on dec. 11, performing tunes both goofy (all i want for christmas is you) and solemn (o come, o come emmanuel).
